About Us

Our Journey Since 2020

Founded in 2020, Agri Direct India emerged with a clear mission: to bridge the gap between modern agricultural technology and traditional farming practices. We recognized the immense potential of drone technology in revolutionizing crop protection and decided to make it accessible to farmers across India.

What started as a vision to help farmers adopt precision agriculture has grown into a comprehensive agricultural technology company. We've successfully completed thousands of spraying operations, helping farmers increase their yields while reducing costs and environmental impact.

Today, we operate across multiple states in India, serving diverse agricultural communities with our advanced drone spraying services and agricultural expertise.

Our Mission

To bridge agriculture and farmers through innovative technology solutions, making precision agriculture accessible, affordable, and sustainable for farming communities across India.
