Use of Drone Spraying in Maize Cultivation:

Improving Yield, Efficiency, and Farm Safety

Published on January 5, 2026 | By AgriDirect India

Maize (corn) is one of the most important cereal crops in India, widely cultivated for food, feed, and industrial use. As maize farming expands and intensifies, farmers face increasing challenges related to pest control, disease management, labor shortages, and input optimization. Drone spraying technology is emerging as a highly effective solution to address these issues.

Why Traditional Spraying Is Challenging in Maize

Maize crops grow tall and dense within a short period, making manual or tractor-mounted spraying difficult and inefficient. Common challenges include:

  • Poor spray penetration in dense canopy
  • Crop damage due to trampling
  • High labor requirement and rising costs
  • Direct exposure of workers to pesticides
  • Uneven application leading to pest resistance

What Is Drone Spraying in Maize?

Drone spraying uses GPS-enabled agricultural drones to apply pesticides, fungicides, herbicides, and micronutrients from the air with high precision. Spray parameters are digitally controlled to ensure uniform coverage across the maize canopy.

Key Benefits of Drone Spraying in Maize Cultivation

1. Uniform Coverage in Tall Crops

Drones spray from above, ensuring consistent coverage of upper and lower leaves, even in advanced growth stages.

Unlike manual or tractor spraying, drones do not trample maize plants or compact soil.

Suitable Applications in Maize Farming

Drone spraying is effective for:

  • Fall armyworm control
  • Stem borer management
  • Fungicide application for leaf blight and rust
  • Foliar nutrition and micronutrient spraying
  • Growth promoters and bio-stimulants

Drone spraying improves return on investment (ROI) by:

  • Increasing overall crop health and uniformity

Conclusion

Drone spraying is transforming maize cultivation by delivering precision, speed, safety, and cost efficiency. For farmers aiming to maximize yield while minimizing operational challenges, drone spraying is a practical and scalable solution.
